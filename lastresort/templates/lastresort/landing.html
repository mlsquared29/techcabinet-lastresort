Last Resort

The Last Resort is a platform that hosts Human vs AI type competitions. A human vs AI competition is a competition where a human is tasked to create problems and their respective solution, following a certain theme. Your problems will then be fed to a LLM, whose answer will be compared with the true given answer. The winner is the one who can "fool" the LLM the most, by coming up with questions that AI cannot answer correctly.
