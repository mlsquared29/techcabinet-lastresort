Last Resort

The Last Resort is a novel competition/benchmark that tests the capabilities of large language models. The main premise for each competition is to create problems and their respective solution, following a certain theme. Your problems will then be feeded to a LLM, whose answer will be compared with the true given answer. The goal is to "beat" the LLM, by coming up with questions that go beyond what they can do.

How It Works

1

Create Questions

Design challenging problems with their solutions and expected answers.

2

AI Testing

Your questions are automatically fed to a large language model for evaluation.

3

Score Calculation

The AI's answers are compared with your expected solutions to calculate your score.

4

Leaderboard Ranking
